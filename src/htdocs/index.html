Secrecy Tool for miro

Basic Instructions

  • Hide Information
    • Tag shapes with a tag Top Secret
    • Click lock symbol in the toolbar and again the lock in the drop out.
    • Provide an arbitrary password
    • Headline and Textinformation of all matching widgets will be encrypted
  • Un-Hide Information
    • Click lock symbol in the toolbar and select the opened lock in the drop out.
    • Provide an correct password
    • Headline and Textinformation of all matching widgets will be shown
